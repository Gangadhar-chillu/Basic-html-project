ANIMALS

THERE ARE TWO TYPES ANIMALS

  1. HERBIVORES
  2. CORNIVORES

HERBIVORES

Herbivores are the animals that have to depend on grass on their survival

  • HORSE

  • ROBBIT

  • ELEPHANT

  • COW

CORNIVORES

Cornivores is an animals that gets food from killing and eating other animals

  • TIGER

  • CROCODILE

  • WOLF

  • LION
